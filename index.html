Who We Are

A Grassroots
Civic Force

ARBR - Amuwo Residents for Better Representation - is a people-powered movement born out of a shared frustration: Amuwo-Odofin deserves better, and we're here to demand it.

We unite ordinary residents, community leaders, and civic-minded citizens to hold leadership accountable, amplify community voices, and push for development that actually reaches people on the ground.

Non-partisan. People-first. Community-driven movement for real change.
Transparent operations - every action taken in the community's interest.
Our Agenda

Four
Pillars

The foundations of the change we're demanding for Amuwo-Odofin. These aren't slogans - they're commitments.

01
👥

People First

Every policy, every decision, every action must serve the people of Amuwo-Odofin. Your voice is the only voice that matters.

02
🎯

Results Driven

No more talk. We focus on measurable outcomes - infrastructure built, problems solved, services delivered on time.

03
🛡️

Integrity

Honesty. Always. We operate with full transparency, zero tolerance for corruption, and unwavering ethical standards.

04
🤝

Accountability

We demand better and we deliver better. Every promise made is tracked. Every leader is held to account - publicly.
